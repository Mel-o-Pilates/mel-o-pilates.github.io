Meet Mel

  • Hi folks! My name is Melanie, I’m a Pilates instructor from Chesterfield. I’m also a professional Musical Theatre performer and am classed as a disabled dancer due to my double Scoliosis (really wonky spine) which causes me a whole host of issues I have to manage. That’s how I found Pilates!

  • I can’t begin to describe how much Pilates has helped me to manage my condition and keep my body functioning as best as it can. It’s so important to me to stay strong and mobile not just for right now, but to give me the best chance of remaining that way in the future. When I started taking Pilates classes at the age of 12/13, I knew I wanted to train as an instructor someday. I wanted to be able to help others with their bodies the way I had been helped.

My Mission

  • My mission is to keep you moving and build your strength! Mobility is everything, and life throws a lot of physical demands at us. Everything from kids, to work, to walking around every day, even just putting your socks on! Being able to move well, stay strong and have good posture leaves you less susceptible to injury and able to go on that walk, do the hoovering without pain or take that dance class.

  • If this sounds like something you want in your life, come and find me! Visit my classes page to book a session or check out my social media pages. Feel free to drop me a DM or email if you have any questions.

I can’t wait to meet you!
